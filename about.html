Welcome

The Rainydays are happy to see that's you have find the way here to us.

Rainydays is one of the Europe's leading company within the marketing for hiking clothes and jackets. We have been operating for over 30 yers and are delivering clothes all over the world.

The first store was opened in Oslo 1990 and already after som few month we was able to open and second stores in Bergen. Three years after the first storage was opened and the growth of the company we started to plan our first store outside of Norway. The thrid store was opened in Sweden in 1993.

Within the coming two years of growth in both Norway and Sweden, Rainydays were able to start to planing for over twenty more stores in both Norway and Sweden. The plan was to have all of this stores in place within 1999.

The success of Rainydays concept was overwhelmed for the company and in 2005 we was ready for the next step for the company. Our plan was to be able to delivery our market and clothes for the hole world and become an world wide business. In January 2008 we opened our first store in USA and within few month we also opened stores in France, Denmark, Italy and in Spain.

In the start of 2010 we opened our world wide delivery from our webbshop. There have been a lot of research and changes over the years but Rainydays absolute comforted with the competition in the market and the biggest winner is our customer.
